Stoplight Approach — Event Registration System

Web Development Software Development Database Design
The Challenge
Stoplight Approach, an organization creating green environments and managing stress, needed an efficient way to manage event registrations and participant data. Manual registration processes were time-consuming, error-prone, and limited their ability to scale their community programs and environmental initiatives.

Digital Voting System

Software Development Security & Compliance Real-time Results
The Challenge
An organization needed a secure, scalable voting system to conduct fair and transparent elections for their community or institution.
